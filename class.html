What to expect if you go to the Philippines

Visiting the Philippines offers a vibrant mix of experiences: stunning beaches, rich cultural heritage, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality. Expect a tropical climate, bustling cities like Manila, and serene rural landscapes. Don't miss exploring islands like Palawan or diving spots in Cebu. Just be prepared for occasional typhoons and heavy rains, especially during the wet season from June to November.
